About Us

We are a small yet very active gaming cafe located at Dublin 1. We hold many tournaments where you can win tons of prizes and have great fun doing it! Our team is very friendly, welcoming and most importantly always there to help. Our aim is to build a great community of players to play along eachother or against in our Game House.
