About Us!

We are a small yet very active Gaming Cafe located down in downtown Dublin. We hold many tournaments where you can win tons of prizes and have great fun doing it! Our team is very friendly, welcoming and most importantly always there to help! Our aim is to build a great community of players to play along eachother or against in our Game House!

What can we provide?

Our Game House is equipped with the absolute best gear you can think of down from the quickest PC's currently available all the way to perfect audio equipment, high refresh rate screens and more!
